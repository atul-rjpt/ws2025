About the Winter School

A five-day program to introduce numerical methods, scientific computing techniques, and machine learning approaches (including Physics-Informed Neural Networks). It mixes lectures, hands-on labs, and expert talks for graduate students, researchers, and professionals.

Key aims include: introduce core concepts, explore integration of ML with scientific computing, and provide hands-on sessions. (Source brochure.)
